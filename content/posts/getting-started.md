This site supports LaTeX math rendering using KaTeX.

## Inline Math

Use single dollar signs for inline math: $E = mc^2$

Or use escaped parentheses: \(a^2 + b^2 = c^2\)

## Display Math

Use double dollar signs for display math:

$$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx = \sqrt{\pi}
$$

Or use escaped brackets:

\[
\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^2} = \frac{\pi^2}{6}
\]

## More Examples

The quadratic formula:

$$
x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}
$$

A matrix:

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{pmatrix}
$$
